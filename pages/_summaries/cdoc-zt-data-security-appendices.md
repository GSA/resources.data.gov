description: "The Zero Trust Data Security Guide Appendix builds upon the
collaborative work of the Federal CISO Council and Federal CDO Council in
advancing the Federal Government’s transition to Zero Trust cybersecurity, as
directed by OMB M-22-09, Moving the U.S. Government Towards Zero Trust
Cybersecurity Principles . Developed through a cross-agency effort involving
more than 30 Federal departments and agencies, the Appendix provides
supplemental context and practical insights to help practitioners
operationalize data security within a Zero Trust framework. Recognizing that
agencies vary in mission, risk tolerance, resources, and cybersecurity
maturity, this Appendix is designed to meet organizations where they are,
offering adaptable guidance that strengthens data security architectures while
fostering closer collaboration between data and security communities. Grounded
in real-world implementation experience and aligned with the core Zero Trust
principle of “never trust, always verify,” the Appendix supports agencies in
protecting Federal data assets and advancing a unified, government-wide
approach to securing the public’s data in an increasingly complex cyber threat
environment.

See also the <a href=\"https://resources.data.gov/assets/documents/Zero-Trust-DataSecurityGuide_RevisedMay2025_CIO.govVersion.pdf\">Zero Trust Data Security Guide</a>"
